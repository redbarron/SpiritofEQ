Background

Jim Vaive
In addition to Jim Vaive’s work with spiritual and emotional intelligence, He is President of Varment Guard Environmental Services Inc, Jim and his partners are leading their business to a steady 10% growth annually. Varment Guard specializes in pest management, wildlife removal & exclusion, termite inspections, and consulting, as well as handling large bird jobs throughout the country. Varment Guard is one of the largest privately held family pest management companies in Ohio, including the surrounding states and consistently wins awards for customer service and attention to detail. This success can be attributed to good integration of departments and smooth work flow.

Due to the company’s expertise and knowledge of the pest management industry, Jim has made appearances on radio and television to talk about how his industry affects your life. Jim’s work with complex jobs and effective problem solving ability has led to the company’s success. His key business focus is coordinating the right people for the most effective outcome. Jim is a member of the Columbus, Ohio and National Pest Control Associations. Among his other accomplishments are the following:

  5. Has been in many prisons around the country
  6. Has been a board member of the Entrepreneurship Institute for 20 years
  7. Was a board member of haven of hope
  8. Is a certified lay speaker in the Methodist church
  9. Has completed Spiritual formation with Spiritual Formation Academy, Upper Room
  10. Has competed Spiritual Direction Training and sits with people on a regular basis
  11. Has held many leadership positions in the church and business
  12. Has been a past member of the board of directors for the National Pest Management Ass.
  13. Has led his company to over 20 years of growth
  14. Has a passion for running, walking and reading
  15. Is certified by “6 Seconds” EQ company and coaches people regularly on their emotional walk/journey
  16. Is certified by “Deep Change” Cindy Wigglesworth SQ 21 Profile and coaches people regularly on their spiritual walk/journey
